# ValueChainHackers

ValueChainHackers is an open research lab and open-source stack to help fix supply chains. We build the tools, the AI, and the automations needed to make real improvements in transparency and sustainability. No buzzwords. Just working tech.

The stack is modular and privacy-first. You can run it yourself. It’s built for students, researchers, companies, and anyone trying to map, clean, and improve value chains. The goal: stop talking about fixing supply chains and actually do it.

---

## The problem

- Supply chains today are full of wicked problems  
- The world needs solutions that are:
  - socially and environmentally impactful
  - financially viable
  - scalable in practice  
- Many want to make a difference, but lack structure and support
  - Businesses
  - Institutions (government, certifiers)
  - Local communities

There is no coordination between these actors. That’s what we aim to change.

## What’s inside

- The full stack:  
  - AI chat + RAG: `Ollama`, `OpenWebUI`  
  - Automation: `n8n`  
  - Data & dashboards: `Grafana`, `Kotaemon`, `JupyterHub`, `RStudio`  
  - Graph database: `Neo4j`  
  - Search & embeddings: `Qdrant`, `SearXNG`  
  - Routing: `Traefik`

- Projects from this year and previous years
- AI tips & tricks for your work
- AI support tools
- Templates you can use in your own research or projects
